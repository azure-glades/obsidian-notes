# Management Roles - Mintzberg 
Categorized 10 roles into 3 categories
**Interpersonal role** -> leading, figurehead (main responsibility, point of contact), liason (contacts and networking)
**Informational role** -> monitoring the team, environment etc, disseminator (informing the team, bringing new roles, giving information), spokesperson (know how to talk, interact with media, news. Good communication)
**Decisional role** -> take decisions, decide direction of project, decide features and discuss with clients

# Skills
**Technical skills** -> knowledge and proficiency (important in first-line manager)
**Human skills** -> working with people (important in all levels)
**Conceptual skills** -> able to describe your idea from abstract idea to working methodolgy/steps to develop a product and seeing if it can be developed/feasible. (Important in top level manager). Identifying opportunity for innovation
# Why study management
