**Table 1: Evolution of Management Thought**

| Era | Time Period | Focus | Key Thinkers | Core Idea |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Classical Era** | 1900 – 1930 | Efficiency, Structure | Taylor, Fayol | Focus on formal processes and productivity. |
| **Human Relations Era** | 1930 – 1950 | People, Motivation, Human Element | Mayo, McGregor, Maslow | People and social relations are central to productivity. |
| **Quantitative & Systems Era** | 1950 – 1980 | Decision Making, System Thinking | Simon, Forrester | Organizations are complex systems; apply models/data. |
| **Contemporary Era** | 1980 – Present | Quality, Agility, Technology | Deming, Peter Drucker, Mintzberg | Continuous improvement, flexibility, and adapting to change. |
