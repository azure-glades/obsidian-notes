Max webber ideas
1. Insistence on following standard rules
2. Systematic division of work
3. Principles of hierarchy to be followed
4. Knowledge & training in application of rules
5. Administrative acts, decisions and rules are recorded in writing
6. Rational personnel administration

Limitations
1. Overconformity of rules - too many rules to follow
2. Buck passing - avoiding decisions/work passing it to others
3. Categorization of queries
4. Displacement of goals
5. No right to appeal
6. Rigid structure
7. Inability to satisfy needs of mature individuals
